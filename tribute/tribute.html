Kobe's phenomenal career in the NBA

  • 1996: Kobe was drafter 13th overall in the NBA draft by Charlotte Hornets. He was quickly traded to the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 1998: Made his first All-Star appearance. He ultimately made 18 appearances.
  • 2000: Won his first of five NBA championship. He alsmo made his first out of nine appearances as an All-NBA Defensive first team.
  • 2006: Scored 81 points in a game against the Toronto Raptors.
  • 2008: Won his lone NBA MVP award
  • 2009: Won his first NBA Finals MVP Award before winning it again in 2010.
  • 2016: In his final NBA appearance, Bryant scored 60 points against the Utah Jazz.
