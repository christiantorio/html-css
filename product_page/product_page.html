About Holding Hearts


Holding Hearts is dedicated to helping families in need during the current pandemic. Our goal is to help families find employment, as well as, various other services.


Holding hearts was created by a group of students that wanted to get involved in their commnunities. At first, they helped familiy and friends by filtering jobs similar to their current skill sets. Through word-of-mouth, more people requested for their assistance. As the pandemic continued on, they realized that they could help more than just family and friends. This is where they realized that they needed to reach out to a larger audience.

Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to help those in need during the pandemic by finding families in need with employment.


VISION: Our vision is to help as many families as we can possibly touch. If employment cannot be found for families, we will offer assistance through other services, such as:

  • Assistance with contacting EDD
  • Determining a new career path
  • Offering classes with volunteers who want to teach new skills, such as, programming or other computer skills
  • Local assistance with other government agencies
